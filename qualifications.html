AYUSH Startup Qualification Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

To register your AYUSH startup, your company must meet the following qualifications:

  • Registered Business: The startup must be a legally registered entity (LLP, Pvt Ltd, or Partnership).
  • Compliance with AYUSH Standards: The startup should adhere to the regulatory requirements of AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, Homoeopathy).
  • Research and Development: The startup should demonstrate a focus on research, development, or innovation in the AYUSH sector.
  • Qualified Personnel: The startup must have qualified professionals such as Ayurvedic doctors, yoga experts, and other skilled personnel in related fields.
  • Proper Infrastructure: The company must have adequate infrastructure for carrying out operations in the AYUSH domain.
  • Product or Service: The startup must either have a product or service related to AYUSH solutions (e.g., medicines, wellness programs, training, etc.).
  • Adherence to Health and Safety Guidelines: The startup must comply with health, safety, and environmental standards set by the regulatory authorities.

Documentation Required

Ensure you have the following documents ready for submission:

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Professional Qualification Certificates of Key Personnel
  • Product or Service Details (e.g., brochures, certificates of testing)
  • Compliance Certifications (if applicable)
  • Health & Safety Compliance Documents

If you meet the above qualifications and have the necessary documentation, you can proceed with the registration process on the AYUSH Startup Registration Portal.
